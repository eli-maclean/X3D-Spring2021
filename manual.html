Manual

In this manual page, you will find instructions on how to use certain elements within our Model page.
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- For use of site on a Personal Computer(Desktop, Laptop):

When going into the Models Page from the home webpage, the Basic Models has a Basic Models dropdown navigational bar at the top of the page. TO SELECT A SPECIFIC MODEL to View/Interact with, Click on the button and select the specific model. This will open a Model on the same page when selected.

TO ACCESS MULTIPLE MODELS, Hold the "CTRL" keystroke & press the Left Mouse Button to select the Models. This will open new windows with the models that are selected.

GOING INTO specific MODEL for INTERACTION, Use the appropriate Mouse Controls instructions found on the page: Holding the Left Mouse Button, move the mouse for rotating the Model; Press Right Mouse Button for to access the "X.ITE Browser" settings related to the Model for more advanced interactions; Use the Mouse Scroll Wheel to Zoom in/Zoom out from into and out of the Model; Holding the Scroll Wheel Button, move the mouse for panning the Model.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- For use of site on a Mobile Device(Phone, Tablet):

IF you are USING GOOGLE CHROME, Tap on the 3 Vertical Dots found right of the URL Address Bar on the Mobile Device. Enable the "Desktop Site" by tapping on the setting to check the box. This will allow full site resolution on the Mobile Device for interaction.

IF you are USING MICROSOFT EDGE, Tap on the 3 Horizontal Dots found at the bottom of the browser right of the Next(" > ") button. Pull the Menu up and look for the "Desktop Site" icon and tap on it to enable the view for that setting.

IF you are USING MOZILLA FIREFOX, Tap on the 3 Vertical Dots found at the bottom of the browser right of the URL Address Bar. Tap on the slider for "Desktop Site" found at the end of the list to enable the appropriate setting.

When going into the Models page from the home webpage, the Basic Models has a Basic Models dropdown navigatinoal bar at the top of the page. To SELECT A SPECFIC Model to View/Interact with, Tap on the specific model to see it on the same page. You can scroll down the list using your fingers or thumb to move the contents up/down.

TO ACCESS MULTIPLE MODELS, Tap and Hold a Model name in the list to bring up a window-menu in center of screen & tap on the "Open in new tab" to open the model in a new window. Repeat the process if more than one Model is to be opened.

GOING INTO specific MODEL for INTERACTION, Use the appropriate Finger Controls and Mouse Controls instructions found on the page.

FOR the FINGER CONTROLS, Pinch inward/outward to zoom in and out of the player for the specific model on the page. Rotate the model with finger(s) to move the camera around as desired on the page.

FOR the MOUSE CONTROLS, Left click and Hold to drag camera around and rotate the model as desired on the page. Right click once on the player to bring up the advanced controls menu for specific selection for the model. Use the scroll wheel on the mouse to zoom in and zoom out of the player for the specific model on the page. Click and hold the scroll wheel on the mouse while moving the mouse around to pan the model on the page.
